Code Breaker

Have you ever wanted to play Mastermind but there is no available "Code-Maker" to play with?
Have you ever wanted to play Mastermind to exercise your mind just whenever you feel like it?
Now you can, with a simple point and click... on your PC, Android tablets, iPad, iPhone, or almost any cellphone.
No Internet Connection required!

The idea of the game is for a player (the Code-Breaker) to guess the Secret Code assigned by the the Code-Maker (the computer).
The Secret Code is a sequence of 4 colored pegs chosen from six colors available.
The Code-Breaker (you) makes a series of guesses - after each guess the Code-Maker gives feedback in the form of black or white pegs:
Black to denote those that are of the right color and in the correct position,
and White for those of correct color but not in the correct position.
Note:The White or Black pegs do not necessarily imply which of the Code-Breaker's pegs are of correct color/position.
